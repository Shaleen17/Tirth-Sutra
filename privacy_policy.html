Privacy Policy

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This policy outlines how The Puranic Path collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide during your sacred journey with us.

1. Information We Collect

To facilitate your pilgrimage, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Personal Identification: Full name, age, and gender.
  • Contact Details: Email address, WhatsApp/phone number, and residential address.
  • Yatra Details: Your chosen pilgrimage, preferred travel dates, and any specific spiritual goals you share with us.
  • Health Information: Any pre-existing medical conditions you voluntarily disclose to ensure your safety and well-being during the yatra.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of organizing and enhancing your pilgrimage experience. This includes:

  • Communication: To contact you regarding your booking, itinerary, and pre-yatra preparations.
  • Bookings: To make necessary reservations with Dharmshalas, transport operators, and other service providers.
  • Customization: To better understand your spiritual needs and tailor the experience where possible.
  • Safety: To be aware of any health considerations that may require attention during the journey.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We hold your trust in the highest regard. We will never sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share necessary information with our trusted partners (such as Dharmshalas and transport providers) for the sole purpose of fulfilling your yatra arrangements. All partners are expected to adhere to similar standards of data privacy.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your data is stored securely, and access is limited to only those team members who require it to perform their duties.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to review, update, or request the deletion of your personal information from our records at any time. To do so, please contact us via the email address provided on our website.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
