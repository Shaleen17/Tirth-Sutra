Terms & Conditions

By booking a yatra with The Puranic Path, you agree to the following terms, which are designed to preserve the sanctity and authenticity of the pilgrimage experience for all.

1. Booking and Payment

A 50% advance payment is required to confirm your booking. The remaining 50% is due 30 days before the date of departure. Bookings made within 30 days of departure require 100% payment at the time of booking. All payments are to be made via bank transfer or the payment link provided.

2. Cancellation Policy

Our yatras are arranged with great care and involve advance bookings with Dharmshalas and transport operators. Therefore, cancellations are subject to the following policy:

  • More than 60 days before departure: 90% refund.
  • Between 30 and 60 days before departure: 50% refund.
  • Less than 30 days before departure: No refund.

3. Pilgrim Conduct (Yatra Maryada)

This is a spiritual journey, not a leisure tour. All pilgrims are expected to maintain a respectful and sattvic demeanor. Consumption of alcohol, non-vegetarian food, and tobacco is strictly prohibited during the entire yatra. Modest attire is required at all sacred sites. The Puranic Path reserves the right to ask any pilgrim to leave the yatra at their own expense if their conduct is found to be disruptive to the spiritual atmosphere.

4. Health and Fitness

Pilgrims are responsible for ensuring they are medically and physically fit for the yatra, especially for journeys involving high altitudes or trekking. Please consult your doctor before booking. You must inform us of any pre-existing medical conditions at the time of inquiry.

5. Limitation of Liability

The Puranic Path acts only as a facilitator for the yatra and is not liable for any unforeseen circumstances such as natural disasters, political unrest, flight/train delays, accidents, or changes in government regulations. While we take utmost care, we are not responsible for any loss of baggage or personal belongings.
