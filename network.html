Our Spiritual Network

Our mission is supported by a network of authentic Ashrams, Maths, and Dharmshalas across Bharatavarsha. These sacred institutions are the foundation of our pilgrimages, providing a pure and simple environment for the sincere yatri.

Connected Centers

Gita Press Dharmshala

Near Vishwanath Temple, Varanasi

A serene stay providing a spiritual atmosphere.

Parmarth Niketan Ashram

Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

Renowned for its Ganga Aarti and spiritual courses.

Govardhan Math

Puri, Odisha

One of the four cardinal maths established by Adi Shankaracharya.

Bangur Dharmshala

Near Dwarkadhish Temple, Dwarka

Providing comfortable lodging for pilgrims visiting Dwarka.

Sringeri Sharada Peetham

Sringeri, Karnataka

The southern math established by Adi Shankaracharya.

Kali Kamli Dharmshala

Haridwar, Uttarakhand

A historic institution providing shelter on the banks of Ganga.
